Physician

This is one of the best paying jobs in medical field.A physician diagnosis and treats the daily and miscellaneous illnesses of the human body. MBBS(Bachelor of Medicine Bachelor of Surgery) degree holders perform as physicians.

Details

Qualifications


• The MBBS course duration is 5.5 years that include one year of internship. People to be selected for MBBS have to pass an Entrance Exams such as CPT, PMT conducted by states and some all India level exams such as AIIMS, AFMS and AIPMT for best colleges in country. The prerequisite for entrance is 10+2/ equivalent having Science subjects.

Top 25 Medical College


•1.All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi
2.Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore

Salery & scope


•According to a January 2016 report from PayScale.com, orthopedic surgeons earned a median salary of $333,097 annually. The total compensation package for a physician or surgeon in orthopedics may increase with yearly bonuses and commissions. Additionally, salary ranges may vary based on work location, employer and experience.

Addition Info


•Certification is not required, but it may increase employment opportunities. A professional designation can demonstrate that a doctor is an expert in a specific area of medicine. Certification by the American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS) involves a thorough process of evaluations and assessments. Continuing education is often required for recertification.
